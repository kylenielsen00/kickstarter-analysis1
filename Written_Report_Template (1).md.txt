# Kickstarting with Excel

## To analyze past theater kickstarter campaigns to help client achieve success. 

### Analysis of Outcomes Based on Launch Date
	It seems that early summer seems to be the best time to launch a successful theater campaign.

### Analysis of Outcomes Based on Goals
	There doesn’t seem to be any real relation on how big the size of the goal is, and if it will be successful or not.

- What are two conclusions you can draw about the Outcomes based on Launch Date?
	It is a good idea to launch a campaign in the early summer.
	Don’t have a goal higher than $45,000.

- What can you conclude about the Outcomes based on Goals?
	The really high goals were least successful.
